Chinese Eateries NYC

Local Authentic Chinese Food. Come try!!

Chinese Eateries NYC is dedicated to assist local and tourists to find authentic Chinese restaurants and shops to eat Chinese food. We are located in 183 Centre St. which is the start point of Chinatown.

Our main focus centers on the 3 Chinatown: Chinatown, Manhattan, 8th Avenue in Brooklyn and Flushing, Main St, Queens. What makes the 3 Chinatown food unique depends on the type of Chinese people who live in the area.

In Chinatown, majority of the Chinese people are from Guangdong Province (southeast China) and Hong Kong the preparation of the food is Cantonese Style. Cantonese Style food are Congee (rice porridge + other ingredients), Noodles (steamed, soup based or stir-fried), and dim sum, with different type of seasoning.

In 8th Avenue, majority of the Chinese people are from Fujian Province (southeast China) closer to water. Many fujianese dishes are seafood. Fujianese food are milder with mixed of sweet and sour taste.

In Flushing, there are different types of Chinese people. Northern Chinese and Taiwanese food are generally more saltier and Southwest Chinese food are spicier. Within the 5 block radius of Main St., you can eat all types of Chinese food making Flushing, Main St. the most popular location to eat Chinese food.

The restaurants, street locations and food listed on our site highlights some of the locations and food local Chinese people typically eat.
